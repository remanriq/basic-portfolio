About Me

Hello all, my name is Rosa Manriquez. I grew up in Soledad, Ca (centeral coast).I attended UC Davis and graduated with a degree in Sociology. I moved to Los Angeles 3 years ago in search of new opportunities. Currently, I am a behavior therapist and I work with children and adults who have developmental disabilities.

I enjoy listening to music, reading personal development books, and traveling. I am a part-time health and wellness coach, so I enjoy helping others feel confident about themselves and love sharing healthy recipes/cooking tips via instagram. I believe health affects our work performance, confidence and overall happiness.
